Lawn Maintenance Services

We offer comprehensive lawn maintenance services to keep your outdoor spaces looking their best year-round. Explore our offerings below to see how ES Lawn Care can help maintain a healthy, vibrant lawn for your property in Winston-Salem, NC.

Mowing

Keep your lawn looking its best with our precise, dependable mowing services. We cater to properties of all sizes in Winston-Salem, NC, ensuring a clean and professional finish every time. Revitalize your lawn with our plugging and reseeding services. We aerate the soil to improve root growth and plant new grass seeds to promote a lush, green lawn. We recommend this service annually to maintain a thick, vibrant lawn that stands up to foot traffic and weather changes.
