About Us

At ES Lawn Care, we’re a family-owned business proudly serving Winston-Salem, NC, and surrounding areas since 2019. What started as a humble operation with just a push mower, weed eater, and small blower has grown into a trusted name in lawn care, built on a foundation of hard work, dedication, and a passion for creating beautiful outdoor spaces. We treat every yard as if it were our own, delivering meticulous care and attention to detail that sets us apart from the typical “mow and go” services.

Our commitment to quality is at the heart of everything we do. We believe in clear communication, prompt service, and treating every customer with the respect they deserve. While we’re not the cheapest option, our pricing reflects the exceptional value we provide—professional-grade results that enhance the beauty and health of your lawn. Whether it’s expert mowing, landscaping, or our signature yearly maintenance services like plugging, reseeding, and fertilizing, we approach every job with the same care and precision we’d give to our own property.

As a family business, we understand the importance of trust and reliability. That’s why we’re dedicated to building lasting relationships with our clients, ensuring your lawn thrives season after season. Choose ES Lawn Care for a lawn care experience that’s as professional as it is personal—because your lawn is our passion.

Our Mission

At ES Lawn Care, our mission is to nurture every lawn into a thriving, beautiful masterpiece that reflects the pride and care we pour into our work as a family-owned business. Since 2019, we’ve been dedicated to treating each yard as if it were our own, providing meticulous lawn care services that prioritize quality, health, and longevity over quick fixes. We believe in fostering trust through open communication, prompt service, and a deep respect for our clients, ensuring a seamless and personalized experience. Our goal is to build lasting relationships by delivering exceptional results that enhance your property’s beauty and value, season after season—because at ES Lawn Care, your lawn isn’t just our job, it’s our legacy.
